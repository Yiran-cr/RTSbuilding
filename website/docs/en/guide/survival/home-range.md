# RTS Home and Range

When survival balancing is enabled, RTS Home decides where you can use RTS features.

If the game says you are too far away, cannot build, or cannot operate remotely, first check the RTS Home position and your current skill range.

## Set RTS Home

Open your inventory and click the RTS Home button at the top of the screen.

On first use, click Set Initial Home. After returning to the RTS view, left-click the target block to confirm. Right-click or press `Esc` to cancel.

After setting it, RTSBuilding calculates usable range around the home position. The home screen shows the current home coordinates, dimension, and build range.

## Range Limit and Skill Progress

RTS operation range is limited by skill tree progress. The starting range is small and expands as related abilities are unlocked.

After full unlocks, the final range limit is controlled by the Maximum Operation Radius setting.

## Move RTS Home

Choose your initial home carefully before unlocking Field Deployment.

After Field Deployment is unlocked, the RTS Home button allows moving the home. The new home position becomes the center of RTS feature range.

## Shared Team Home

When Team Sharing is enabled, players on the same team share the RTS Home anchor.

If a server enables survival balancing and team sharing, the team should decide the base center first, then set RTS Home, so different members do not end up with inconsistent operating ranges.
