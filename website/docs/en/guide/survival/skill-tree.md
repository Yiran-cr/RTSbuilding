# Skill Tree and Unlocks

When survival balancing is enabled, RTSBuilding's core abilities must be unlocked gradually through the skill tree.

If some buttons are unavailable after opening RTSBuilding, or the UI says an ability is locked, the current world probably has survival balancing enabled.

## Enable Survival Balancing

If you added the mod yourself, or the world does not enable survival balancing by default, you can turn it on manually.

Press `G` to open the RTS interface, click the gear button in the upper-right corner, and open the settings panel.

Find Survival Balancing, enable it, and save settings.

In multiplayer, server configuration can force this setting.

## Open the Skill Tree

Open your inventory and click the RTS Skill Tree button at the top of the screen.

Skill tree nodes show their current state:

- Locked: prerequisite abilities are not unlocked yet.
- Missing Materials: prerequisites are met, but your inventory does not contain the required materials.
- Unlockable: materials are enough. Click the node to spend materials and unlock it.
- Completed: the ability is already unlocked.

## Recommended Core Unlock Order

For core abilities, a good order is:

1. Camera Core: unlock RTS overhead control.
2. Storage Binding: unlock binding for chests, machines, or storage systems so the bottom bar can browse and use their items.
3. Unlock branches as needed: Remote Placement for building, Remote Mining for gathering, Crafting Terminal and JEI Transfer for base management.

## What Changes After Unlocking

After an ability is unlocked, related buttons appear in the RTSBuilding top bar, bottom bar, or inventory entry.

If the UI does not update immediately after unlocking something, close and reopen RTSBuilding, or reopen the inventory and check the corresponding entry.

## Shared Team Progress

When Team Sharing is enabled in settings, players on the same team share RTS skill progress and RTS Home anchors.

Teams come from FTB Teams when available. Without FTB Teams, vanilla scoreboard teams are used.
