# FAQ

## Q: I bound a chest, AE system, or other storage, but I cannot store items into it. What should I do?

First check whether you right-clicked the target while binding it.

Right-click binding is extract-only. RTSBuilding can take items from that storage, but it cannot put items back into it.

If you want the storage to support both storing and extracting:

1. Open RTSBuilding.
2. Click the Storage Binding button in the top bar.
3. Aim at the currently bound chest, AE interface, or storage controller, then left-click once to cancel that binding.
4. Aim at the same target and left-click again to bind it normally.
5. Check that the top hint says storage is bound.

After rebinding with left click, that storage can both accept items and provide items.
