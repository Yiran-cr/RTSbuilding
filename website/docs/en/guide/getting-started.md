# First Steps

## Open RTSBuilding

Press `G` in game by default to open RTSBuilding and enter the overhead building view.

Press `G` again, or press `Esc`, to close the RTS interface.

## UI Layout

RTSBuilding has three main UI areas:

- Top bar: switch modes and open quick build, ultimine, settings, and tutorials.
- Bottom bar: view stored items, select blocks, use blueprints, and craft remotely.
- Side floating window: appears beside container or machine screens and helps move items into RTS storage.

## First-Time Setup

First, bind a chest as RTS storage.

1. Open RTSBuilding.
2. Click the Storage Binding button in the top bar.
3. Aim at a chest in the world.
4. Left-click the chest.
5. Check that the top hint says storage is bound.
6. Check that the bottom bar shows the chest contents.

After binding storage, left-click a block in the bottom bar, aim at a placement position, and right-click to place it.

## Survival Mode Limits

If some buttons are missing, survival balancing may be enabled. Open your inventory and click the RTS Skill Tree button at the top of the screen, then spend materials to unlock storage binding, remote placement, remote mining, and other abilities.

Survival balancing also requires an RTS Home. Open your inventory, click the RTS Home button at the top of the screen, and set a home position. RTSBuilding uses that home as the center of your allowed RTS range.

For details, read Skill Tree and Unlocks and RTS Home and Range in Survival Systems.

## Tutorials and Hints

Click the `i` button in each UI area to open its tutorial.

The top hint updates as you play. It shows the current mode, selected item, bound storage state, and step-by-step quick build prompts such as waiting for point A or point B.
